Welcome To Oasis Grandstand Yamuna Expressway

The Oasis Grandstand, developed by Oasis Group, is a stunning residential complex located along the Yamuna Expressway in Sector 22D, Greater Noida. Spanning an expansive **9.26 acres**, this meticulously planned project features **1262 thoughtfully designed apartments** spread across **8 towers**. Offering a harmonious blend of luxury and tranquility, Oasis Grandstand is the ideal destination to turn your dream of a perfect home into reality.

  • Complete comfort and convenience for residents.
